Tic Tac Tie

updates | Apr 14, 2016

Tic Tac Tie was an accidental pun made while discussing the program’s computer logic it used against the player. It was suppose to be simple and aim to always create a draw. Hence the ‘tie’.

Unfortunately I did find one win condition it can’t seem to beat.

Which is okay as it was my first ‘AI’ I’ve made to play against someone.

I didn’t do much design on this one. I may revist it. Main goal was to see how quickly I could go from concept in my head to fully functional. I had to get up early take a long car ride downtown. I felt I had all the kinks and issues worked out in my head. How i’d style it, the html layout and the javascript.

So it felt great to write HUGE chunks of it with little testing before finishing. Still a lot of micro tweaks, but overall it works great.

Tic Tac Tie, as usual you can find the code on my github.
